Mf Kårhouse

In mid-2025, I joined the student organization MF Kårhuse, where I collaborated with a team of students to create marketing materials across several channels connected to Linköping University. This work was primarily voluntary, driven by a shared goal of improving communication and engagement within the student community. I initially worked as a graphic designer for Studentlivet.se, a platform focused on making student life more accessible and engaging. A mascot concept, Studde, had already been introduced before I joined, but had not yet been fully implemented. I played a key role in establishing how the mascot was visually and tonally used across content, helping define its expression and integration. Studde quickly became a well-received element, with posts featuring the character contributing to stronger engagement and a more cohesive visual identity. Over time, I expanded my role to include work with Kombo, a platform focused on student housing. Here, I worked as both a graphic designer and project leader. As part of this, I created Kubbe, a mascot inspired by Kombo’s logo, which forms a heart - this lead to Kubbe being designed as a heart-shaped character. The goal was to increase engagement and add personality to the platform’s communication. Kubbe was featured in a series of animations and posts, often placed in humorous or relatable scenarios such as being scammed or navigating student housing challenges, which helped make the content more approachable and shareable.
