This was an assignment for the course TNGD41, where to goal was to use gestalt psychology. In this design I used the law of similarity. My thought process was the following: the use of the law of similarity can be reflected in how I chose to make one of the pigeons orange - as to point to difference. Further, the other pigeons are meant to be observing the orange pigeon, not judging it - but merely, observing.
