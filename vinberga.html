For: Vinberga Allmekano

Part of the course TNGD45 consisted of brand identity, and learning how to build it. In a team of three people we had the opportunity to put this to practice by working with the brand Vinberga Allmekano, and creating a graphic profile for them - in areas such as web, print, textile print and more. Our team worked closely with the brand and extensive research was done in regards to target-group and in what way we could help them achieve what they wanted. The result was a graphic profile, unique and adapted to their brand and values. Below you can see their old logo, and the new one we designed.
